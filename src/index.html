Folio

Designed with the executive in mind, the Folio keeps the essentials fashionably organized. This sleek, genuine leather portfolio is comprised of a soft microsuede lining, an integrated viewing and typing stand, and a sturdy snap closure to keep it all secure.

- Leather snap closure strap
- 4card slots, ID card slot
- Integrated viewing and typing stand
- Soft microsuede lining
- Fits iPad 2/3/4

Works with:iPad Air, iPad Mini, Nexus 7, Kindle Fire
